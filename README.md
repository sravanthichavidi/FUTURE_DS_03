## Objective
To analyze marketing funnel data and evaluate conversion performance across
different stages and marketing channels.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

## Analysis Performed
- Funnel stage-wise visitor and conversion analysis
- Calculation of conversion rates using SUMIF
- Identification of conversion drop-offs in the funnel
- Channel-wise conversion performance analysis
- Cost vs Revenue and profitability analysis
- Data visualization using funnel and column charts

## Key Insights
- Awareness stage attracts the highest number of visitors but has a low conversion rate
- Major conversion drop-off occurs between Consideration and Intent stages
- Google Ads and Email Marketing show better conversion performance
- Purchase and Retention stages generate higher profitability
- Retention campaigns deliver higher revenue with lower cost

## Conclusion
This analysis provides actionable insights to optimize marketing strategies,
improve conversion rates, and enhance overall business performance.
